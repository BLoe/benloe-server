## Component Specifications

### 1. League Settings Summary

**Component**: `LeagueSettingsSummary`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="league-settings-summary"`

Shows your 9 categories with highlights on non-standard ones.

### 2. Player Value Shifts

**Component**: `PlayerValueShifts`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="league-value-shifts"`

Lists biggest risers and fallers compared to standard rankings.

**Data TestIDs**:
- `league-value-shifts`
- `league-value-riser-{rank}`
- `league-value-faller-{rank}`

### 3. Positional Impact Chart

**Component**: `PositionalImpactChart`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="league-position-impact"`

Bar chart showing value change by position.

### 4. League-Adjusted Rankings Table

**Component**: `LeagueAdjustedRankings`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="league-adjusted-rankings"`

Searchable/filterable table comparing standard vs your league rankings.

**Data TestIDs**:
- `league-adjusted-rankings`
- `league-rankings-search`
- `league-rankings-position-filter`
- `league-rankings-row-{playerId}`

### 5. Exploitable Edges

**Component**: `ExploitableEdges`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="league-exploitable-edges"`

AI-generated tips for leveraging league format differences.
