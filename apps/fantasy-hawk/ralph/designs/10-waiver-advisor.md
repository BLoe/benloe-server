**Component**: `WaiverStatusPanel`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="waiver-status"`

Shows priority position, FAAB balance, next processing time.

### 2. Pickup Recommendations

**Component**: `PickupRecommendations`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="waiver-recommendations"`

### 3. League Activity Feed

**Component**: `LeagueActivityFeed`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="waiver-activity"`

### 4. FAAB Spending History

**Component**: `FAABSpendingChart`
**Test ID**: `data-testid="waiver-faab-history"`

**Component**: `TopAcquisitionsTable`
